  1. Harry James Potter

    2. Harry James Potter (b. 31 July 1980) is a half-blood wizard, one of the most famous wizards of modern times. He began attending Hogwarts in 1991 and was Sorted into Gryffindor House.

    After the war, Harry became an Auror and helped reform and revolutionise the Ministry of Magic

  2. Hemione Granger

    3. Minister Hermione Jean Granger (b. 19 September, 1979) was a Muggle-born witch. She began attending Hogwarts in 1991 and was Sorted into Gryffindor House. Hermione possessed a brilliant academic mind

    She later found employment with the Ministry of Magic, furthering the cause for the better treatment of house-elves. Afterward she was promoted to the Department of Magical Law Enforcement.

  3. Ronald Weasly

    4. Ronald Bilius "Ron" Weasley (b. 1 March, 1980) was a pure-blood wizard, the sixth and youngest son of Arthur and Molly Weasley. Ron began attending Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in 1991 and was Sorted into Gryffindor House.

    After the war, Ron became an Auror and helped reform and revolutionise the Ministry of Magic.
